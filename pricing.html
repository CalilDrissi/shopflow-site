Frequently asked questions

What counts as a "run"?

Each time a workflow executes — whether manually triggered, scheduled, or via webhook — counts as one run. A workflow with 10 nodes that processes 50 products is still just 1 run.

Can I bring my own AI API key?

Yes. ShopFlow supports any OpenAI-compatible API. You provide your own API key — we never see or store it. Works with OpenAI, Anthropic, local models, and custom endpoints.

Is there a free trial for paid plans?

The Free plan is permanent — no trial period. You can use it as long as you want. When you're ready for more workflows and runs, upgrade to Starter or Pro.

Can I connect multiple Shopify stores?

Free allows 1 store. Starter supports 3 stores. Pro supports unlimited stores. All managed from a single ShopFlow dashboard.

What happens if I hit my run limit?

Workflows will pause until your limit resets at the start of the next billing cycle. You'll get a notification when you're approaching the limit. Upgrade anytime to increase it.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. No contracts, no cancellation fees. Downgrade to Free at any time and keep your workflows — they'll just pause if you exceed the Free plan limits.
